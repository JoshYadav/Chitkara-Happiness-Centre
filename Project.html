Welcome to Chitkara University

CHITKARA HAPPINESS CENTRE

The Chitkara Happiness Centre stands as a beacon of well-being, providing a nurturing and supportive environment where individuals can explore and enhance their overall wellness. Rooted in the principles of positive psychology and the renowned PERMA well-being model, the centre offers an array of programs, workshops, and resources designed to cultivate happiness, resilience, and personal growth.

Our dedicated team empowers individuals to develop self-awareness, build positive relationships, and effectively manage stress. Through a diverse range of happiness courses and sessions, we aim to instill in our students a profound understanding of the significance of well-being and happiness in life. Recognizing that both joy and sorrow are natural aspects of life, the centre encourages the choice of happiness and equips students with the tools to embrace life's challenges with grace.

At the heart of our philosophy lies the belief that flexibility is key to happiness, and we work diligently to help students nurture this choice. The HH Dalai Lama Centre for Happiness is not just an institution; it is a sanctuary where every being is enriched with joy, knowledge, optimism, success, and love. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge and foster their creativity, enabling them to find happiness while overcoming obstacles in society. While traditional education prepares students for their future careers, our institution of happiness prepares them for life, ensuring they embark on their journeys with resilience, compassion, and a profound sense of well-being.

Objectives of Chitkara Happiness Centre

  • To enhance the personal characteristics of students by developing the traits of Mindfulness, Emotional Intelligence, and Resilience.
  • Creating a welcoming and inclusive environment that supports personal growth and fosters positive relationships.
  • To improve the overall well-being of students by using the PERMA Model to evaluate their happiness levels and implementing evidence-based interventions to enhance each PERMA component.

PERMA PROJECT
